This activity makes use of the IOnSceneTouchListener in coorespondence with the IPinchZoomDetectorListener and PinchZoomDetector classes in order zoom the camera in and out based on two fingers pressing and moving closer together and further apart on the display.
The pinch-to-zoom functionality requires either the ZoomCamera or SmoothCamera in order to properly apply zoom factors to the camera.

Installation:
1. Import the ApplyingPinchToZoom class to your project.
2. Replace your AndroidManifest.xml's startup activity with ".ApplyingPinchToZoom"
